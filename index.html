Privacy Policy & Data Protection

1. Data Collection & Purpose

ZENTRA AI Group ("Zentra," "we," "our") collects personal information strictly for the purpose of delivering security consultation, system design, installation, and monitoring services. Information collected through our website contact forms — including name, phone number, email, asset type, and location data — is used exclusively to initiate and manage security engagements. We do not collect data from minors under the age of 18.

2. Surveillance Data Handling

All surveillance footage, access logs, sensor data, and threat reports generated by ZENTRA AI systems are classified as Confidential Client Data. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest using military-grade AES-256 encryption protocols. Surveillance data is stored on dedicated, air-gapped local servers and/or encrypted cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 Type II compliance. Under no circumstances is client surveillance data sold, shared, licensed, or made accessible to third parties — including marketing firms, data brokers, or analytics companies.

3. Florida State Compliance

ZENTRA AI operates in full compliance with the Florida Information Protection Act (FIPA), Florida Statutes §501.171, and all applicable state and federal regulations governing electronic security systems, surveillance data, and consumer privacy. Our low-voltage contracting operations are licensed under the State of Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation (DBPR). All installations adhere to the National Electrical Code (NEC) and local building authority requirements.

4. Non-Disclosure Agreements

Every client engagement is protected by a binding Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) executed prior to any site survey, system design, or installation work. This NDA covers all proprietary information including but not limited to: property layouts, security system architecture, camera placement maps, access control configurations, network infrastructure details, and threat assessment reports. ZENTRA AI employees and subcontractors are bound by confidentiality agreements as a condition of engagement.

5. Data Retention & Destruction

Client surveillance data is retained for periods agreed upon in individual service contracts, typically ranging from 30 to 180 days depending on system configuration and client requirements. Upon contract termination or client request, all data is permanently destroyed using Department of Defense (DoD) 5220.22-M standard data sanitization methods. Certificates of destruction are provided upon request.

6. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting client information, ZENTRA AI will notify affected clients within 24 hours of confirmed discovery, in compliance with FIPA requirements. Our incident response team maintains documented breach response protocols, including forensic investigation, containment, client notification, and regulatory reporting procedures.

7. Your Rights

Clients and website visitors have the right to: (a) request access to personal data held by ZENTRA AI; (b) request correction of inaccurate data; (c) request deletion of personal data, subject to contractual and legal retention requirements; (d) opt out of marketing communications at any time. To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer through the secure contact form above or by requesting a direct line through our consultation process.

This policy was last updated on March 24, 2026. ZENTRA AI reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated to active clients directly. This policy applies to all ZENTRA AI websites, services, and client engagements within the United States.
